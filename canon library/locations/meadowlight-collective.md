# Meadowlight Collective

**Type:** Intentional community, ~2 hours west of Philadelphia (Brooklyn ↔ Meadowlight ≈ 3 hrs driving)

**Details:**
- Cluster of weathered buildings around a central farmhouse; gardens and a greenhouse
- Hand-painted sign on a dirt driveway
- Where Hank tends his great-grandmother's Fish Peppers after fleeing the pressure

**Story Significance:** The only Book 1 setting outside Brooklyn. Where Jasper finds Hank (Ch23) via the Fish Pepper / nursery trail, and where Hank agrees to come back for the final dinner.

# McCarren Park Greenmarket

**Type:** Weekly farmers market
**Location:** McCarren Park, Williamsburg

**Operating rule (hard):** **Saturday ONLY, morning–early afternoon.** Emma cannot be here on a Sunday; other days it's closed.

**Details:**
- Larger and more crowded than McGolrick — the bigger Saturday market
- Hank's hot sauce stall was here too (his Saturday market)
- After Hank's disappearance his stall is quickly filled by a new vendor (jarred salsa — "José's Jarred Salsas")
- Near Sofia's cheese shop (15–20 min walk from McGolrick)

**Story Significance:** Emma visits for the first time in Ch11 (Meal 2) and finds Hank's spot already replaced — the market has moved on. Contrast with McGolrick, where his spot stays empty.

## After Book 1: The Case of the Missing Hot Sauce (October)

### World State

**The Supper Club:** Formalized as a named tradition at the end of Book 1. Group agreed to continue with rotating hosts (all meals were at Emma's in Book 1). Next book will have a different host arrangement.

**The Farmers Market:** McGolrick Park's Down to Earth Farmers Market. Hank's stall was vacant throughout Book 1. By the end, Hank has returned and filed a proper trademark for his hot sauce with pro bono legal help.

**The Mogul (Garrett Pike):** His investors pulled out after the swarm strategy. The waterfront restaurant location is on indefinite hold. The cease & desist was formally withdrawn. Pike is not destroyed — his operation is stalled, and he may resurface.

**Emma's secret & finances:** Emma's private income source stays **opaque** — it was never named on the page in Book 1, and its reveal is deferred to a later book. Pike's retaliation was an **economic squeeze** (cancelled private clients, a killed article, editors going cold), not exposure; the C&D named no secrets. As Pike retreated, that pressure lifted. What changed for Emma is private and interpersonal: she let **Olivia** in on her financial precarity and the shame beneath her proud exit from Bistro Lavande. She never went public with anything. Money is still tight, but she's no longer carrying the fear alone.

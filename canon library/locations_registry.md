**Address:** Williamsburg, near McCarren Park  
**Type:** Railroad apartment, small but character-filled  
**Details:**
- Cramped but beloved kitchen, the heart of the space
- Dining table that seats six with effort
- Eclectic décor, some inherited from grandmother
- Fire escape with a view of limited usefulness

**First Appearance:** Book 1, Chapter 1

**Details:**

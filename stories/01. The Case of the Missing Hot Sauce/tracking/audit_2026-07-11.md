- **Problem:** Jasper removes the bottle Wednesday night at the end of the emergency dinner (Ch14–16). Emma's writing session is Thursday. She can't be looking at a bottle that's already on its way to Pennsylvania. ### 1.2 🔴 "Meadowlight Collective" is named on Thursday, collapsing the Ch19–23 search
- **Where:** `m3_17...:176` — Jasper says the name on the Thursday Amtrak, sourced from a Lancaster County nursery woman (`:148`).
- **Problem:** The entire five-day Pennsylvania search (Ch19–23) is built around *discovering* that name — the bartender at Murph's reveals it Sunday night after Jasper nearly gives up (`timeline_ledger.md:31`, `character_matrix.md:64–65`). - **Fix (prose):** Make the Ch17 lead vaguer — "a collective out past the city where growers share land," no name. Reserve "Meadowlight" for the Ch22 bartender reveal. (Also fixes the internal geography slip: Ch16 says "upstate from Philly," Ch17 says "west" — canon is west/Lancaster Co.)

### 1.3 🔴 Brenda's warning text fires twice, on two different nights
- **Where:** `m2_10_loose_lips.md:70` (Friday Oct 9, "a text she'd send at 11 PM that would sit unread… all night") vs. `m2_12_coming_to_a_boil.md:156–160` (Sunday Oct 11, "his phone buzz with a text from Brenda as he walked home").

This is the load-bearing dramatic-irony device, so the doubling matters. Tracking collapses it to one text carried Fri→Sun, so the duplication lives in the prose.

Cleanest: keep Friday's missed-calls/text as the breach beat in Ch10; recast the Ch12 coda as *escalation* ("another text, the third since Friday") so Jasper's weekend-long obliviousness is deliberate. Or drop the Friday 11 PM text and let the single warning land Sunday.

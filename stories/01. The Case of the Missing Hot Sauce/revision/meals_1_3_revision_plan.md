# Book 1 — Meals 1–3 Revision Plan (the platform for Meal 4)

Build-spec for the front-half rework. Derived from `tier1_high_level.md` and `tier2_meal_level.md`. Goal: get Meals 1–3 solid and internally consistent so Meal 4 can be drafted on a stable base. **This plan is for sign-off before any prose is rewritten.**

---

- **Feed-based POV** throughout.
- **Emma owns Book 1**, ensemble still evolves.
- **Secret stays opaque.** OnlyFans deferred to a later book. Emma's on-page vulnerability = shame over the proud Bistro Lavande exit vs. precarious freelance reality, confided to Olivia. Villain leverage = economic squeeze.

- **Put Pike on-page at least once.**

## Cross-meal threads to coordinate
These are the wires that run between meals. Each must be set up early and paid off later.

1. **Deferred reveal → Meal 3 discovery.** Hold one reveal back from the Ch12 midpoint so the crisis still has something to uncover.
2. **Pike seed → Pike on-page.** Plant in Meal 1/Ch12, pay off with a face-to-face in Meal 3.
3. **Precarity seed → Olivia confide.** Seed Emma's fired/precarity shame in Meals 1–2, pay it off as the private confide in Meal 3.
4. **Economic squeeze.** The retaliation mechanism: assignments cancelled, editor kills her article. Seed late Meal 2 / open Meal 3.

## Open creative choices (need your sign-off)

**A. Which reveal do we defer? (recommend: the mogul's identity.)**
In the current draft, Jasper's Ch12 call delivers the mogul's name as the triumphant button. Recommendation: have Brenda get spooked and go vague *before* naming him (she realizes who's involved and backs off — which also motivates the breach). The group ends Ch12 with the LLC, patents, motive, and Paolo pattern, but **not the who**. Meal 3 then discovers/confirms the identity, and that discovery can *be* the on-page Pike beat (choice B). Benefits: real Meal 3 discovery, a more genuinely "false" victory, and Jasper's contribution becomes double-edged (he caused the breach and didn't even get the prize), which strengthens his Meal 3 guilt. Cost: rewrites Jasper's Ch12 reveal. *Alternative if you'd rather keep Ch12 intact: defer only the patent's full breadth (that it would criminalize Hank selling his own sauce) — smaller change, weaker payoff.*

**B. How does Pike appear on-page? (recommend: he approaches Emma directly, charming.)**
Per his profile he visits markets, knows food, sends wine, charms before the lawyers. Recommendation: a Meal 3 beat where Pike approaches Emma in person — warm, reasonable, "I hear you've been asking about Hank, let me buy you a coffee" — which reads as quietly menacing because she knows what he is. This can double as the identity reveal (choice A). Seed him earlier as the well-dressed man at the market (Ch1 and/or the Ch12 man at Dorothy's stall, already present).

**C. Where do we add investigation friction? (recommend: Sofia guarded + one trace dead-end.)**
Make Sofia initially wary before she opens up (she's seen Paolo destroyed; caution fits). And/or give Noah's trace a dead-end layer before the Delaware breakthrough. Pick one or both.

**D. Pike backstory contradiction. (recommend: update the profile to the noodle-cart origin.)**
Sofia's "little noodle cart in Red Hook fifteen years ago… he used to be one of us" is thematically stronger (community lifted him; he pulled up the ladder) than the profile's "managed restaurants, opened an East Village farm-to-table in 2011." Golden rule favors the prose. Recommendation: update `book1_garrett_pike.md` to incorporate the humble Red Hook street-cart origin.

# Sofia's Cheese Shop

**Type:** Neighborhood cheese shop (Williamsburg, near McCarren)
**Owner:** Sofia

**Operating rule:** Open Tuesday–Sunday; closed Mondays. Sofia does not text during closed hours. (Emma cannot drop in on a Monday.)

**Details:**
- A Williamsburg fixture for twenty years; survived three waves of gentrification by being too good and too stubborn to close
- Narrow and deep, a full-length glass case; sensory anchor of sharp cheddar, funky blue, fresh mozzarella, and yeasty bread from the back
- Sofia wraps wedges with practiced speed, apron dusted with flour
- Emma is a regular customer

**Story Significance:** Where Emma learns Hank got "a big offer to go legit" and hears the **Paolo precedent** (Ch11). In the revised draft, Sofia is **initially guarded** before she opens up, and notes that people have been around asking about Hank. She also gestures at the mogul's humble origin ("used to be one of us… a noodle cart in Red Hook").

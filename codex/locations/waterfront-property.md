# Waterfront Property (Williamsburg)

**Type:** Vacant commercial space

**Details:**
- The site of [Garrett Pike](../antagonists/book1_garrett_pike.md)'s planned waterfront restaurant, filed through LLC #2847
- Projected December 1 opening; expedited permit timeline
- By the end of Book 1 the project is on indefinite hold (investors pull back)

**Story Significance:** The "what was this all for" of the mystery — the prize Pike was clearing vendors to build. Pike's "legacy project," and the thing the swarm campaign ultimately stalls.

# Elijah's Apartment

A quiet, orderly one-bedroom in **Bed-Stuy**, near the Classon Ave G stop. Elijah lives alone and keeps the space spare because "disorder cost energy he couldn't afford to waste." Streetlights along Classon Avenue; brownstones out the window.

**Story significance:** Appears only in Ch8 — Elijah's solo POV, where he takes Olivia's call, seeds the LLC to the group chat, and builds the financial model. **The group has never been inside.** Elijah has never hosted, and his home staying unknown to them is a deliberate seed for the **Book 6** hosting payoff (see `series_plan.md` and `continuity.md`). Distinct from [Elijah's Midtown Office](elijahs-office.md).

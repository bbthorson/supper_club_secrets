# NYPL — Rose Main Reading Room

**Type:** Research location (New York Public Library)

**Operating rule:** Open Monday–Saturday; closed Sunday. Hours Mon–Thu 10am–8pm, Fri–Sat 10am–6pm. Oliver cannot research at NYPL on a Sunday; if he does library work on a Sunday it's a different branch or he's at home.

**Details:**
- Vast, cathedral-like, the iconic green reading lamps; smells of old wood and aging paper
- Oliver has a regular desk — third row from the back, second seat from the aisle, near a power outlet

**Story Significance:** Where Oliver discovers the anonymous LLC #2847 filing (Book 1, Ch. 6) — the break into the investigation. His first "flutter of excitement" about the case.

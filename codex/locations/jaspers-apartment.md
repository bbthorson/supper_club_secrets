# Jasper's Apartment

**Address:** Chelsea, off Eighth Avenue (Manhattan)
**Type:** Chaotic one-bedroom — "looks like a thrift store and a bookstore had a fight"

**Details:**
- Mismatched furniture, leaning stacks of books (urban beekeeping to Cold War espionage), a half-finished Williamsburg Bridge jigsaw on the table
- The only organized thing: his vinyl collection, alphabetized in milk crates with hand-written dividers
- Surprisingly nice for the chaos — partially funded by family money he doesn't discuss

**Story Significance:** Jasper's solo chapter (Ch10) where he calls Brenda. The fact that it's a comfortable **Chelsea (Manhattan)** apartment is the featherlight class cue used in the Meal 3 fracture — the source of the cushion (old money) stays undisclosed until Book 6.

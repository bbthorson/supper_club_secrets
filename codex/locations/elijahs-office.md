# Elijah's Midtown Office

**Type:** Mid-tier investment bank; glass-walled conference room, 40th floor

**Operating rule:** Standard finance hours, occasional weekend work. Elijah works late on a Sunday only when a deadline forces it — overtime is the signal that something is up, so a late-Sunday appearance needs a reason.

**Details:**
- Where Elijah builds his financial model during a lunch break (Ch8), rediscovering a curiosity he hasn't felt since freshman econ at UPenn

**Story Significance:** The model valuing an artisanal hot-sauce brand at low-to-mid six figures supplies the **motive** that convinces the group (and Noah) the case is real.
